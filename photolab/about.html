About

I have loved photography ever since I was a baby clown. My parents gave me a camera for my third birthday and I have had a camera in my hand ever since. I got my bachelor's degree in Photography with an emphasis in Fast Food back in 2010 and I have over 30 years of professional experience. I have won the Golden Chicken Nugget Award 7 years consecutively for my photo contributions.

One of my favorite things about photography is the opportunity I have to watch people during their special moments. Watching a couple get married inside of the PlayPlace or witnessing a child take their first bite of a Hot 'n Spicy McChicken really touches my soul and inspires me to be better.

Outside of photography I am a part time private detective trying to catch the hamburglar. I also enjoy watching Netflix, playing with my dog, and practicing the banjo.

Thank you so much for looking through my work and I look forward to serving you in the future!
